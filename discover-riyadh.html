Welcome

Welcome to Riyadh

Riyadh isn't just a city — it's a statement. A place where centuries of heritage meet the unstoppable momentum of Vision 2030. It's a city that invites you to look closer, go deeper, and stay longer. From world-class museums and ancient fortresses to cutting-edge architecture and vibrant dining scenes, Riyadh rewards every kind of traveller.

Desert & Nature

Explore the Rub' al Khali — the Empty Quarter

The world's largest continuous sand desert is a place of extraordinary beauty and silence. Join a guided dune safari at golden hour and witness a landscape that has inspired poets and explorers for millennia.

Plan your desert trip

History & Heritage

Lose yourself in the Al-Murabba Historical Palace

Step into the halls where modern Saudi Arabia was shaped. This beautifully restored palace offers an intimate window into royal life and the country's 20th-century transformation. Adjacent museums bring the full story to life.

Explore heritage

Arts & Culture

The JAX District — Riyadh's creative heartbeat

Housed in a repurposed oil facility, JAX has become the city's most exciting arts and culture district. Independent galleries, design studios, film screenings, and concept stores fill the industrial space with creative energy.

Discover JAX

Gastronomy

Dine across the world without leaving the city

From traditional Saudi cuisine in heritage restaurants to world-class contemporary dining rooms, Riyadh's restaurant scene is one of the most exciting in the Middle East. Every meal is an adventure.
